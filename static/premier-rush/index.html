What you'll make

You'll make a 2 to 3 minute video that engages your viewers on the topic that you're talking about.

It'll have the following features:

  • Video and clips edited together
  • Your voiceover narration

Here are a couple of examples:

  • Disney - The Magic of Animation
  • PlayTime - Controlled Chaos

What you'll learn

  • How to create an engaging script
  • How to edit a video in Adobe Premier Rush

What you'll need

  • A topic that you're interested in
  • Adobe Premier Rush
  1. Find some images and video clips that you want to use.

You will need plenty of images and a few short clips.

Avoid long clips because they become boring for the viewers - the focus is your voiceover.

⚠️ Put all the images and clips into a folder before your start

Premier Rush doesn't make copies of your files, so once you start you cannot move or delete the files until after you've completely finished.

  1. Open Adobe Premier Rush and start a new project

Now you're ready to get editing!

Steps

  1. Click on the blue plus (+) in the top left corner
  2. Find the folder where you saved your clips
  3. Click on each clip that you want to add
  4. Click the blue Add button at the bottom

Steps

  1. Click on the clip you want to move to highlight it yellow
  2. Click and drag the clip to the place you want it to be

Steps

  1. Click on the clip you want to split to highlight it yellow
  2. Move the blue play head line to where you want the split to be
  3. Click on the scissors (✂) icon on the left

Steps

  1. Click on the clip you want to change to highlight it yellow
  2. Click on the crop/rotate button in the top right
  3. Select either Fill or Fit at the top

Steps

  1. Click on the clip you want to change to highlight it yellow
  2. Hover over either the start or the end of the clip until the mouse cursor changes to an arrow
  3. Click and drag to lengthen or shorten the clip

Steps

  1. Click on the clip you want to add a transition to to highlight it yellow
  2. Click on the Effects icon in the top right
  3. Click on the transition you want to apply

Steps

  1. Move the blue play head line to where you want the graphic to be
  2. Click on the Graphics icon in the top right
  3. Click the Add Graphic button
  4. Choose the graphic you want to use
  5. Click Add at the bottom

Steps

  1. Click on the Control Tracks button in the bottom right corner to show the video and audio tracks
  2. Click on the Microphone icon to add a track for recording audio
  3. Click on the red Record button to start recording (after a countdown)

Move to a quiet place to record your voiceover. Close the windows and doors, and turn off any fans, heat pumps, etc.

Do your voiceover in parts. Don't worry about getting the timing right and if you mess up, just go back to the start of the sentence or paragraph, not the start of the whole script..

You can always edit, delete, or move the voiceover around after you're done.

Steps

  1. Click on the Share tab in the top right
  2. Name your file appropriately
  3. Check that the quality is high
  4. Click the blue Export button
  5. Wait for the video to finish exporting
  6. Click on the View in Finder (Mac) or Show in Explorer (Windows) link to view your file
  7. Watch the video to check that it's what you want
  8. Submit your finished video essay
