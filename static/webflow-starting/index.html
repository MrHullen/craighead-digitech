What you'll make

You'll make a multipage website using the Webflow Editor.

It'll have the following features:

  • A Header
  • A Footer
  • Main content

What you'll learn

  • How to create elements of a web page using Webflow's Editor.

What you'll need

  • A topic to create your site about
  • A Webflow account

Steps

  1. Login to Webflow
  2. Go to your Dashboard
  3. Click New site
  4. Choose Blank Site

Elements

  1. Click the Add icon and drag a new Section onto the page.
  2. In the Section Settings, change the tag to Header.
  3. Click the Add icon and drag a new Heading onto your Section.
  4. Change the heading text to what you want.

Style

  1. Click on the Heading and switch to the Section.
  2. Add padding spacing to the top and bottom of your Section.
  3. Change the background colour to what you want it to be.
  4. Click on the Heading and choose the typography you want: colour, alignment, and font.
  5. Add more fonts from Google Fonts if you like.
  6. Preview your Header to make sure it looks the way you want it to to your users.

Publish point

You've added a complete feature to your website. You should Publish it.

Elements

  1. Click the Add icon and drag a new Section onto the page.
  2. In the Section Settings, change the tag to Footer.
  3. Click the Add icon and drag a new text block onto your Section.
  4. Change the text to what you want.

Style

  1. Click on the Footer and switch to the Section.
  2. Add padding spacing to the top and bottom of your Section.
  3. Change the background colour to what you want it to be.
  4. Click on the Footer and choose the typography you want: colour, alignment, and font.
  5. Add more fonts from Google Fonts if you like.
  6. Preview your Footer to make sure it looks the way you want it to to your users.

Publish point

Layout

  1. Click the Add icon and drag a new Section onto the page.
  2. In the Section Settings, change the tag to Main.
  3. Click the Add icon and drag Columns into the Section.

Spacing

  1. Click on the Columns and switch to the Section.
  2. Add padding spacing to the left and right of your Section.

Content

  1. Click the Add icon and drag a Heading into one of your Columns.
  2. Change the text to what you want, and adjust the typography: colour, alignment, and font.
  3. Change the Heading type to H2.
  4. Click the Add icon and drag a Paragraph into one of your Columns.
  5. Change the text to what you want, and adjust the typography: colour, alignment, and font.
  6. Click the Assets icon and upload a new image, then drag it into the other Column.

Repeat

  1. Apply the same steps again to create another set of columns.
