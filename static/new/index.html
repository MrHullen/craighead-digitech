If you would like to be extra prepared and utilize some of Google's other resources to help make school easier, the following section gives you some ideas.

These extra apps are all available from the sidebar in Gmail.

Craighead is a Google school, but that doesn't mean you can't use other apps. The suggestions below can be swapped out for any app that you already use or would prefer. You can use Apple's Calendar, Reminders, and Notes instead, for example.

Google Calendar

A calendar app can help you keep track of everything, if you've got a lot going on.

  • Timetable - there's a link in Kamar to add your timetable directly into your calendar
  • Practices - add recurring events to remind you about sports practice, music lessons, or anything else that happens regularly
  • One-off events - whenever you get told about something that's easy to forget, just add it to your calendar: field trips, shared lunches, mufti days

You can install Google Calendar on your phone so that you can check it whenever you want (outside of school).

Google Tasks

Todo lists remember things for you, so that you can concentrate on more important things.

  • Homework - when your teacher gives you homework, add it to your todo list with a reminder for when you get home
  • Extra gear - set a reminder the night before practice to pack your sports gear

You can install Google Tasks on your phone so that you can check it whenever you want (outside of school).

Google Keep

Note-taking apps help you save important information, links to helpful sites, or ideas you have for later.

  • Tutorials - store the link to a good video tutorial
  • Codes - note your printing code if you struggle to remember it, or you don't use it often
  • It's important to never store your passwords in a note-taking app
  • Use a dedicated password manager instead
  • How tos - if you figured out a way of doing something and you want to remember it for next time, jot it down in a note
